## Feature request

### Overview

Support TimestampNTZ data type for Delta tables
### Motivation
Currently this datatype is supported in Spark 3.3, but Delta throws an UnsupportedException.

### Further details
Spark documentation for timestampNTZ - [timestamp without time zone](https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/SPARK-35662). It's a local timestamp without the time zone information.
