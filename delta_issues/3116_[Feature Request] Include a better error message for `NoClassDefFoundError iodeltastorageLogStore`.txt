## Feature request

### Overview

Depending on your environment and its internet access, the `delta-storage` dependency might not be properly included with `delta-lake` during execution.

Several users have already encountered this (#1103 , #1152). Including a better error message, with steps to resolve the issue, would be helpful.

### Motivation

Make it easier for users to fix the rare problem of `delta-storage` dependency not being downloaded.
